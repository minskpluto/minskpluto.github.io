What is gradient?

Gradient is a slope of a line. The higher the gradient of a graph at a point, the steeper the line is at that point. A negative gradient means that the line slopes downwards.

Parallel lines have same gradient

When two gradients are multiplied and equal to -1, they are a perpendicular line
