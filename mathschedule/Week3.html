Definition of x intercept - The x-coordinate of a point where a line, curve, or surface intersects the x-axis.

Definition of y intercept - The point where the line crosses the vertical y-axis

Definition of domain - The set of values of the independent variable for which a function or relation is defined.

Definition of range - The range of a function is the complete set of all possible resulting values of y after we have substituted the domain.
